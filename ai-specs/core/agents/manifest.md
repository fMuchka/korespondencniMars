# The AI Agents Manifest (Friendly Edition)

## 1. The Liaison

- **Role:** Your Friendly Guide & Translator
- **Spec:** `liaison.md`
- **Input:** Natural ideas ("I want a pony")
- **Output:** **Story Card**

## 2. The Coordinator

- **Role:** Strategy, History & Team Routing
- **Spec:** `coordinator.md`
- **Input:** Story Card
- **Output:** **The Task** (Assigned to appropriate agent)

## 3. The Specialist Pair

- **QA Buddy**: The Coach. Writes tests, guides the loop. (`qa_buddy.md`)
- **The Maker**: The Builder. Writes the code. (`maker.md`)

## 4. Quality Lead

- **Role:** Final Polish & Curator
- **Spec:** `quality_lead.md`
- **Input:** Finished Task
- **Output:** Thumbs Up/Down
